Welcome to F(orgotten) Risk. It is basic.

Documentation

This project is to remind myself of all the things I have forgotten and am forgetting about risk. It will end up being a one stop shop for the fundamentals and allow me to chuck out all my course notes from the MTF. It will also be the limit of what I will hold in (or near) memory and be the extent to which I will profess competence in discussions with practicioners. I am writing it only for myself and so the explanations will be such that I understand them. If it helps others then I am delighted but that will be a side effect only. I will cover the following:

  • Historic Volatility
  • Pricing a Vanilla European Call (and Put) with Black Scholes
  • Put-Call Parity
  • Implied Volatility
  • Greeks and impact of fundamentals on option prices
  • Monte Carlo simulation
  • VaR
